启动三个应用放到了：/usr/local/djsigncenter/artifacts，分别对应auth、back、gateway；里面有各自的shell脚本
启动nacos，现在是单机的：
cd /usr/local/nacos/nacos/bin
nohup ./startup.sh -m standalone &

启动sentinel：
cd /usr/local/sentinel_1.6.1
nohup ./startSentinel.sh &


启动rabbit：
启动插件
./rabbitmq-plugins enable rabbitmq_management
启动服务
./rabbitmq-server -detached


cd /usr/lib/rabbitmq/bin


启动项目：
启动三个服务：
cd /usr/local/djsigncenter/artifacts/auth
nohup ./startauth.sh &


cd /usr/local/djsigncenter/artifacts/back
nohup ./startback.sh &

cd /usr/local/djsigncenter/artifacts/gateway
nohup ./startgateway.sh &
